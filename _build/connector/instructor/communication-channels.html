Communication Channels

Biweekly Instructor Meetings

We will be holding connector instructor meetings every two weeks. These meetings are announced through the mailing list. We will post notes for those who are not able to attend. These meetings will focus on topics that are of interest to the connector instructors.

Mailing List

You should have been added to the connector instructor mailing list/google group. If you are not sure whether you have been added, you can view all your memberships at groups.google.com. To email all instructors on the mailing list, you can send an email to ds-connector-instructors@lists.berkeley.edu.
